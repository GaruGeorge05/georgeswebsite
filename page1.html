What is Terraria?

Terraria is a 2d sandbox game that revolves around digging, fighting, building, and exploring. The game is noted for its classic exploration-adventure style of gameplay, similar to games such as the Metroid series and Minecraft. Terraria was released on May 16, 2011 and updates are still being released to this day! Terraria can be played on any platform, such as PC, Console, and Mobile too. You can play singleplayer or multiplayer in Terraria, which adds more fun to the gameplay. It recently just got an update on the mobile platform which released the long awaited 1.3 content.

First night basics

When you make your first world, you're gonna start off with three basic tools. The copper shortsword, copper pickaxe, and the copper axe. These will be your main weapons for the night. So first, before the night comes, chop down trees to collect wood. You'll need about 200 peices of wood. With the wood, make a work bench, one of the many crafting stations of Terraria which expand your crafting limits. Make some wooden walls, about 120, and start making your house. You can go beyond and create an amazing house but for now, we'll start with something simple. To start, place 15-20 of wood on the grass to create the base for your house. On both sides of the house, place 5-6 blocks of wood going up . After that, once both sides of the house are on the same level, connect them together. You should have a box now. To get out of the house, take down 3 blocks of wood, above the ground level, to make space for a door. Make your door and place it in the empty space you just made and tada! You've made a house. All that's left now is a light source to mobs don't spawn in so to do that, just make and place some torches in your house and you are set for your first night.

Once you have enough of one ore, you can start smelting it into bars to create weapons and armour. When you have better loot and a full set of armour, you may start preparing for your first boss battle.

First Boss Prep

For your first boss, you can either choose to fight King Slime, or the Eye of Cthulu. Generally, people fight King Slime first because he is the easiest to kill with beginner weapons. King Slime is a giant blue slime who jumps towards you to attack. An easy way to fight King Slime is by placing rope vertically until you can barely see the ground. This way, King Slime cannot reach you AT ALL, cheesing the whole battle. Spawn him, go up the rope, use a bow, and defeat him. If that's too easy for you, you can fight the Eye of Cthulu. It's a giant eye ball that thrusts towards you. To prep for this boss, make an arena by making a long bridge of wooden platforms. Make and place campfires on the bridge for extra health regeneration. It is recommended to use long ranged weapons for this boss, such as yoyo's, boomerangs, or a bow. With your weapon of choice ready, spawn in the Eye of Cthulu and start running accros your bridge. While running across your bridge, the Eye will start following you so attack him. Once he is at half health, the Eye of Cthulu will spin around and pupil will turn into a giant mouth. At this stage, he thrusts faster but his defense weakens. Keep your eyes open and try to dodge every attack. Remember to use your potions wisely, since there is a one minute cooldown! After you defeat the Eye of Cthulu, he will drop some gold coins, potions, and either Demonite or Crimtane, depending on your world. Good Job!

Exploring the Corrupt/Crimson

In your world, there will be a special biome that can spread thorughout your world if left unattended. Your world can either have the Corrupt biome, or the Crimson biome. The Corrupt biome is a purple biome littered with gaps leading to a big cavern full of demon orbs and demon altars. This biome holds special enemies such as the eater of souls, devourers, and its special boss, the Eater of Worlds. The Crimson on the other hand is a red biome with dome-like structures that lead down to a circular room full of extenions that hold demon hearts and Crimson alars. This biome also has its own kind of enemies, such as face monsters, blood crawlers, and its special boss, the Brain of Cthulhu. Both biomes also have their unique soundtracks.
